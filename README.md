## About the Author

**Nik Bear Brown** teaches business ethics, AI ethics, and management at Northeastern University. He holds an MBA from Northeastern (alongside a PhD in Computer Science from UCLA and an MS in Information Design and Data Visualization). Through [Bear Brown & Company](https://www.bearbrown.co/), he advises early-stage AI and machine-learning startups; the ethical questions of the field's current deployments are exactly what his consulting and his teaching converge on.

He is the founder of [Humanitarians AI](https://www.humanitarians.ai/), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that supports international graduate students through production-scale AI projects with real-world stakes. He is the author of the *with LLMs* textbook series.

He works in Boston. [nikbearbrown.com](https://www.nikbearbrown.com) · [irreducibly.xyz](https://irreducibly.xyz) · [skepticism.ai](https://www.skepticism.ai)
