## Figure 9.1: Four sectors, four pressures

**Placement:** Chapter opening, after Fowler scene.

**Description:** Four-panel chart. Panel 1 (entrepreneurship): founder personality scales into firm culture. Panel 2 (advertising): information asymmetry between persuader and audience. ## Figure 9.4: Risk-pooling failure under climate amplification

**Placement:** Concept 3, after risk-pooling introduction.

**Description:** Two-panel diagram. Left: classical risk pool (independent, geographically dispersed losses). Right: catastrophic correlated pool (single event hits many policyholders simultaneously, exceeds reserves). Arrow from left to right labeled "climate amplification."

**Pedagogical purpose:** Private-sector insurance's actuarial assumptions assume independence; climate-amplified events violate the assumption.

**Style:** Two-panel diagram with simple shapes, plain serif.
