# Figure briefs — Chapter 11: Epilogue

## Figure 11.1: Before-and-after composite

**Placement:** Chapter opening, after Gupta scene.

**Description:** Three-panel composite. Each panel: "before" portrait of a successful career, "after" portrait of post-failure standing.
- Panel 1: Rajat Gupta as McKinsey global managing director (1994-2003) → federal felon (2012).
- Panel 2: Bill Swanson as Raytheon CEO and *Unwritten Rules* author → exposed plagiarist (2006).
- Panel 3: GM senior leadership 2014 (industry leaders) → defendants in $900M settlement (2015).

**Pedagogical purpose:** Reputations are slow to build, fast to lose; the chapter is about why.

**Style:** Three-panel composite, plain serif labels, no color hierarchy. Each "before" and "after" with a one-line description.

## Figure 11.3: Personal mission statement template

**Placement:** Concept 3, after the four-step framework.

**Description:** Worksheet-style table. Four rows for the four steps.
- Row 1: Five values (numbered 1-5).
- Row 2: Source of each value (where you learned it).
- Row 3: Challenge each value will face.
- Row 4: Specific action committed for each.
- Final box: One-paragraph integrated statement combining the above.

**Pedagogical purpose:** The mission statement is operational: short investment of clarity that compounds over a career.

**Style:** Worksheet table, plain serif, lined for handwritten completion.
