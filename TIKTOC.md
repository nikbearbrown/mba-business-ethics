# Business Ethics: with LLMs
## Compact Tik TOC Architecture

**Book type:** MBA course textbook  
**Status:** Retrospective architecture for developed content  

## Core Thesis

This book teaches business ethics as practical judgment under pressure: identifying stakeholders, incentives, duties, harms, tradeoffs, and accountability before a decision becomes a scandal.

## Primary Reader

MBA students, managers, founders, and professionals preparing to make or evaluate organizational decisions.

## Learning Arc

1. **Ethical frameworks:** consequences, duties, virtues, justice, care, and rights.
2. **Business contexts:** markets, labor, consumers, data, environment, governance, and AI.
3. **Decision practice:** cases, memos, stakeholder analysis, disclosure, and accountability.

## Chapter Architecture

Each chapter should begin with a decision case, force a stakeholder map, apply multiple frameworks, and end with a defensible recommendation.

## Terminal Capability

The reader can analyze business dilemmas, name tradeoffs honestly, and write a decision memo that survives ethical scrutiny.

## Adoption Risks

- Cases can become opinion exercises without analytic structure.
- AI examples must avoid confident moral shortcuts.
- Controversial topics require explicit framework discipline.
